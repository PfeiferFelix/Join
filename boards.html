To Do

Here you can find all tasks that are planned to be done. You can add new tasks by clicking the "+" button above. Drag and drop tasks to move them to the next stage of your project.

In Progress

Here you can find all tasks that are currently in progress. You can move tasks to this section by dragging them from the "To Do" section. Keep track of your team's progress and update task statuses as needed.

Awaiting Feedback

Here you can find all tasks that are awaiting feedback. You can move tasks to this section by dragging them from the "In Progress" section. Review feedback and update task statuses accordingly.

Done

Here you can find all tasks that are completed. You can move tasks to this section by dragging them from the "Awaiting Feedback" section. Celebrate your team's achievements and keep track of completed tasks.
